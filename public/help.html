Networking

How to Connect

1. Scan QR: Use the Scan tool to capture a student's digital badge. This instantly saves them to your network.

2. Share Profile: Tap "My Profile" to reveal your own QR code. Let others scan you to exchange details.

Participation

Events & Schedule

• Registering: Click "Register" on any event to add it to your personal schedule.

• Check-in: When you arrive, scan the Event's QR code to mark yourself as "Attended".

Insights

Understanding Data

• Engagement Score: This metric tracks your activity across campus events.

• Network Growth: View the "Analytics" tab to visualize your connections.

Guidelines

Community Standards

• Professionalism: Keep profile bios and messages appropriate for a school setting.

• Respect: Zero tolerance for harassment. Admins can revoke access for violations.

Troubleshooting

Frequently Asked Questions

My camera isn't opening.

Ensure you have allowed browser permissions for the camera. Refresh the page after changing settings.

Where is the Logout button?

If you are on a smaller laptop screen, you can scroll down inside the sidebar to reveal it.

Can I edit my profile photo?

Yes. Go to "My Profile" and click the camera icon on your avatar.
