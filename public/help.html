Networking

How to Connect

1. Scan QR: Go to the "Scan/Upload" page to scan another student's digital badge. This instantly saves them to your network.

2. Share Profile: Tap "My Profile" to show your own QR code. Let others scan you to share your socials and portfolio.

Participation

Events & Schedule

• Registering: Browse the "Events" feed. Click "Register" to add an event to your personal schedule.

• Check-in: When you arrive at a venue, use the Scan tool to scan the Event's QR code to mark yourself as "Attended."

Insights

Understanding Data

• Engagement Score: This number rises as you attend events and make new connections.

• Network Growth: View the "Analytics" tab to see a timeline of your connections and which events gave you the most value.

Troubleshooting

Frequently Asked Questions

My camera isn't opening for scanning.

Ensure you have allowed browser permissions for the camera. On iOS/Android, go to Settings > Safari/Chrome > Camera and set it to "Allow".

I can't see the "Logout" button on my laptop.

We recently updated the sidebar layout! You can now scroll inside the navigation menu on the left to reveal the bottom buttons.

How do I edit my profile photo?

Go to "My Profile" and click the small camera icon on your avatar. You can upload a new JPG or PNG file (max 5MB).
